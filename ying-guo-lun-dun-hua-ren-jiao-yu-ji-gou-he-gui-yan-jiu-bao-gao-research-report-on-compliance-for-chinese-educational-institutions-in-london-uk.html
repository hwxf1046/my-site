    ## 执行摘要

     

    伦敦作为全球教育中心和英国的首都，长期以来是华人教育机构设立分支机构和开展合作的重要基地。英国拥有完善的教育监管体系、严格的质量标准和国际认可度高的教育体系，为教育机构提供了良好的发展环境。然而，教育机构运营涉及复杂的许可申请、质量保证、学生权益保护和合规义务，对华人教育机构构成了挑战。本报告基于凤凰律师网服务伦敦华人教育机构的丰富经验，深入分析教育机构合规的核心问题，并提供系统性的合规建议。

     

    **Executive Summary**

     

    As a global education center and capital of the United Kingdom, London has long been an important base for Chinese educational institutions to establish branches and conduct cooperation. The UK has a comprehensive education regulatory system, strict quality standards, and a highly internationally recognized education system, providing a good development environment for educational institutions. However, the operation of educational institutions involves complex licensing applications, quality assurance, student rights protection, and compliance obligations, posing challenges for Chinese educational institutions. Based on extensive experience of Phoenix Lawyer Network in serving Chinese educational institutions in London, this report provides an in-depth analysis of core compliance issues and offers systematic compliance recommendations.
