    一、研究背景与投资环境

    Research Background and Investment Environment

     

    东京作为日本首都和亚洲重要城市，拥有稳定的房地产市场、完善的法律体系和优质的基础设施。对于华人房地产投资者而言，东京提供了良好的投资机会，但涉及复杂的法律要求和程序。本报告基于对东京房地产投资法律环境的深度分析，为华人投资者提供系统性的房地产投资法律实务指南。

     

    As Japan's capital and an important Asian city, Tokyo has a stable real estate market, comprehensive legal system, and high-quality infrastructure. For Chinese real estate investors, Tokyo provides good investment opportunities but involves complex legal requirements and procedures. This report is based on an in-depth analysis of Tokyo's real estate investment legal environment, providing systematic real estate investment legal practice guidelines for Chinese investors.
