    ## 执行摘要

     

    雅加达作为印尼的首都和东南亚最大经济体，近年来成为华人能源投资的重要目的地。印尼拥有丰富的能源资源、快速增长的能源需求和政府的大力支持，为能源投资者提供了良好的投资环境。然而，能源投资涉及复杂的许可申请、环保要求、社区关系和合规义务，对华人投资者构成了挑战。本报告基于凤凰律师网服务雅加达华人能源投资者的丰富经验，深入分析能源投资的法律风险，并提供系统性的风险防控建议。

     

    **Executive Summary**

     

    As capital of Indonesia and the largest economy in Southeast Asia, Jakarta has become an important destination for Chinese energy investment in recent years. Indonesia has abundant energy resources, rapidly growing energy demand, and strong government support, providing a good investment environment for energy investors. However, energy investment involves complex licensing applications, environmental requirements, community relations, and compliance obligations, posing challenges for Chinese investors. Based on extensive experience of Phoenix Lawyer Network in serving Chinese energy investors in Jakarta, this report provides an in-depth analysis of legal risks in energy investment and offers systematic risk prevention recommendations.
