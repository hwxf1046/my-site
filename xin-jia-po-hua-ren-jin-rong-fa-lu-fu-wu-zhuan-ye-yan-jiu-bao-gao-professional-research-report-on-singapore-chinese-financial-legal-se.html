    一、研究背景与金融中心地位

    Research Background and Financial Center Status

     

    新加坡作为亚洲重要的金融中心，拥有完善的金融法律体系和监管框架，吸引大量华人金融机构和投资者。在新加坡开展金融活动涉及复杂的法律要求，包括金融机构设立、金融产品合规、反洗钱要求、数据保护等多个方面。本报告基于对新加坡金融法律环境的深度分析，为华人金融机构和投资者提供系统性的金融法律服务指南。

     

    As an important financial center in Asia, Singapore has a comprehensive financial legal system and regulatory framework, attracting a large number of Chinese financial institutions and investors. Conducting financial activities in Singapore involves complex legal requirements, including financial institution establishment, financial product compliance, anti-money laundering requirements, data protection, and other aspects. This report is based on an in-depth analysis of Singapore's financial legal environment, providing systematic financial legal service guidelines for Chinese financial institutions and investors.
