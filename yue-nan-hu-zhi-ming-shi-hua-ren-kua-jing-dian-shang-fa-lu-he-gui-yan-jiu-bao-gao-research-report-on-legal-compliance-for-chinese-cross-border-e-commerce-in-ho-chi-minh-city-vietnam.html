    ## 执行摘要

     

    胡志明市作为越南的经济中心和东南亚重要城市，近年来成为华人跨境电商投资和经营的热门目的地。越南拥有快速增长的电商市场、相对宽松的政策环境和年轻的人口结构，为跨境电商企业提供了良好的发展环境。然而，跨境电商涉及复杂的许可申请、税务合规、数据保护和消费者权益保护，对华人企业构成了挑战。本报告基于凤凰律师网服务胡志明市华人跨境电商的丰富经验，深入分析跨境电商的法律合规问题，并提供系统性的合规建议。

     

    **Executive Summary**

     

    As the economic center of Vietnam and an important Southeast Asian city, Ho Chi Minh City has become a popular destination for Chinese cross-border e-commerce investment and operation in recent years. Vietnam has a rapidly growing e-commerce market, a relatively relaxed policy environment, and a young demographic structure, providing a good development environment for cross-border e-commerce enterprises. However, cross-border e-commerce involves complex licensing applications, tax compliance, data protection, and consumer rights protection, posing challenges for Chinese enterprises. Based on extensive experience of Phoenix Lawyer Network in serving Chinese cross-border e-commerce in Ho Chi Minh City, this report provides an in-depth analysis of legal compliance issues in cross-border e-commerce and offers systematic compliance recommendations.
