    一、研究背景与就业环境

    Research Background and Employment Environment

     

    日本作为世界第三大经济体，拥有独特的劳动法律体系和就业文化。对于在日本工作的华人而言，了解和遵守日本的劳动法规至关重要，不仅关系到自身权益保护，也影响到职业发展和在日本的生活质量。本报告基于对日本劳动法律环境的深度分析，为华人劳动者和雇主提供系统性的劳动法律实务指南。

     

    As the world's third-largest economy, Japan has a unique labor law system and employment culture. For Chinese people working in Japan, understanding and complying with Japanese labor regulations is crucial, not only relating to protection of their own rights and interests but also affecting career development and quality of life in Japan. This report is based on an in-depth analysis of Japan's labor legal environment, providing systematic labor legal practice guidelines for Chinese workers and employers.
