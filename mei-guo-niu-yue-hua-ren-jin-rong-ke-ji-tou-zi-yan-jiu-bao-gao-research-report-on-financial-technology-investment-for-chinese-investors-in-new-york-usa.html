    ## 执行摘要

     

    纽约作为全球金融中心和科技创新中心，近年来成为华人金融科技投资的重要目的地。美国拥有完善的金融监管体系、活跃的风险投资环境和成熟的科技生态系统，为金融科技投资提供了良好的发展环境。然而，金融科技投资涉及复杂的监管要求、数据保护、知识产权保护和合规义务，对华人投资者构成了巨大挑战。本报告基于凤凰律师网服务纽约华人金融科技投资者的丰富经验，深入分析金融科技投资的核心问题，并提供系统性的投资建议。

     

    **Executive Summary**

     

    As a global financial center and technology innovation center, New York has become an important destination for Chinese financial technology investment in recent years. The United States has a comprehensive financial regulatory system, an active venture capital environment, and a mature technology ecosystem, providing a good development environment for financial technology investment. However, financial technology investment involves complex regulatory requirements, data protection, intellectual property protection, and compliance obligations, posing huge challenges for Chinese investors. Based on extensive experience of Phoenix Lawyer Network in serving Chinese financial technology investors in New York, this report provides an in-depth analysis of core issues in financial technology investment and offers systematic investment recommendations.
