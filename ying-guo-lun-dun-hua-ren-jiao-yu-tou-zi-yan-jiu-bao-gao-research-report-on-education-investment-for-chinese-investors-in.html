    ## 执行摘要

     

    伦敦作为全球教育中心和英国的首都，长期以来是华人教育投资的重要目的地。英国拥有世界一流的教育体系、完善的教育监管体系和国际认可度高的教育质量，为教育投资提供了良好的发展环境。然而，教育投资涉及复杂的许可申请、质量保证、学生权益保护和合规义务，对华人投资者构成了挑战。本报告基于凤凰律师网服务伦敦华人教育投资者的丰富经验，深入分析教育投资的核心问题，并提供系统性的投资建议。

     

    **Executive Summary**

     

    As a global education center and capital of the United Kingdom, London has long been an important destination for a Chinese education investment. The UK has a world-class education system, a comprehensive regulatory framework, and high-quality education quality, providing a good development environment for education investment. However, education investment involves complex licensing applications, quality assurance, student rights protection, and compliance obligations, posing challenges for Chinese investors. Based on extensive experience of Phoenix Lawyer Network in serving Chinese education investors in London, this report provides an in-depth analysis of core issues in education investment and offers systematic investment recommendations.
