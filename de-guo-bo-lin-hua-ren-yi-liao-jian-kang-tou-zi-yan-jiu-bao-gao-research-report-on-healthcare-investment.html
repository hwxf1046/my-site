    ## 执行摘要

     

    柏林作为德国的首都和欧洲重要的医疗中心，近年来成为华人医疗健康投资的重要目的地。德国拥有先进的医疗技术、完善的医疗体系和严格的监管标准，为医疗健康投资提供了良好的环境。然而，医疗健康投资涉及复杂的许可申请、质量保证、数据保护和合规义务，对华人投资者构成了挑战。本报告基于凤凰律师网服务柏林华人医疗健康投资者的丰富经验，深入分析医疗健康投资的核心问题，并提供系统性的投资建议。

     

    **Executive Summary**

     

    As capital of Germany and an important European medical center, Berlin has become an important destination for Chinese healthcare investment in recent years. Germany has advanced medical technology, a comprehensive healthcare system, and strict regulatory standards, providing a good environment for healthcare investment. However, healthcare investment involves complex licensing applications, quality assurance, data protection, and compliance obligations, posing challenges for Chinese investors. Based on extensive experience of Phoenix Lawyer Network in serving Chinese healthcare investors in Berlin, this report provides an in-depth analysis of core issues in healthcare investment and offers systematic investment recommendations.
