    ## 执行摘要

     

    米兰作为全球时尚之都和意大利的经济中心，长期以来是华人时尚产业投资和经营的重要基地。意大利拥有世界领先的时尚产业、完善的知识产权保护体系和成熟的商业环境，为时尚企业提供了优质的发展环境。然而，时尚产业涉及复杂的知识产权保护、品牌管理、供应链合规和国际贸易，对华人企业构成了独特挑战。本报告基于凤凰律师网服务米兰华人时尚企业的丰富经验，深入分析时尚产业的法律风险，并提供系统性的防控策略。

     

    **Executive Summary**

     

    As the global fashion capital and economic center of Italy, Milan has long been an important base for Chinese fashion industry investment and operation. Italy has a world-leading fashion industry, a comprehensive intellectual property protection system, and a mature business environment, providing an excellent development environment for fashion enterprises. However, the fashion industry involves complex intellectual property protection, brand management, supply chain compliance, and international trade, posing unique challenges for Chinese enterprises. Based on extensive experience of Phoenix Lawyer Network in serving Chinese fashion enterprises in Milan, this report provides an in-depth analysis of legal risks in the fashion industry and offers systematic prevention strategies.
