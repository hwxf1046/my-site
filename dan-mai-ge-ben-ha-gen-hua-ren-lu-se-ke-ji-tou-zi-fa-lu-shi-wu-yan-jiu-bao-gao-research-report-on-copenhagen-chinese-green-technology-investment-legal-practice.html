    一、研究背景与绿色科技环境

    Research Background and Green Technology Environment

     

    哥本哈根作为丹麦首都和全球绿色科技中心，拥有世界领先的绿色技术生态系统、完善的法律体系和政府支持政策。对于华人绿色科技投资者而言，哥本哈根提供了优质的绿色科技投资环境，特别是在清洁技术、循环经济、绿色建筑等领域。本报告基于对哥本哈根绿色科技投资法律环境的深度分析，为华人绿色科技投资者提供系统性的投资法律实务指南。

     

    As Denmark's capital and a global green technology center, Copenhagen has a world-leading green technology ecosystem, comprehensive legal system, and government support policies. For Chinese green technology investors, Copenhagen provides a high-quality green technology investment environment, especially in fields such as clean technology, circular economy, and green buildings. This report is based on an in-depth analysis of Copenhagen's green technology investment legal environment, providing systematic investment legal practice guidelines for Chinese green technology investors.
