    一、摘要

     

    随着全球化进程加速，国际婚姻日益普遍，但涉及的法律问题复杂多样。不同国家的婚姻法、家庭法和继承法存在显著差异，给国际婚姻当事人带来诸多挑战。本文将深入分析国际婚姻家事中的主要法律痛点，并提供专业的解决方案。

     

    Abstract

     

    With the acceleration of globalization, international marriages are becoming increasingly common, but the legal issues involved are complex and diverse. Significant differences in marriage law, family law, and inheritance law among different countries bring numerous challenges to parties in international marriages. This article will deeply analyze the main legal pain points in international marriage and family matters and provide professional solutions.
