    一、研究背景与劳动环境

    Research Background and Labor Environment

     

    瑞典以其先进的劳动法律体系、完善的社会保障和高质量的工作环境著称。对于在瑞典工作的华人而言，了解瑞典劳动法不仅关系到自身权益保护，还影响到职业发展和生活质量。本报告基于对瑞典劳动法律环境的深度分析，为华人劳动者和雇主提供系统性的劳动法律实务指南。

     

    Sweden is known for its advanced labor law system, comprehensive social security, and high-quality working environment. For Chinese people working in Sweden, understanding Swedish labor law not only relates to protection of their own rights and interests but also affects career development and quality of life. This report is based on an in-depth analysis of Sweden's labor legal environment, providing systematic labor legal practice guidelines for Chinese workers and employers.
