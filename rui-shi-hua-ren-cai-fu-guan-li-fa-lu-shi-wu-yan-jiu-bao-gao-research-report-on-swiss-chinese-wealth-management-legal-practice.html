    一、研究背景与金融中心地位

    Research Background and Financial Center Status

     

    瑞士作为全球财富管理中心，拥有完善的金融法律体系和严格的银行保密制度。对于华人高净值人士而言，瑞士提供了优质的财富管理服务，但同时也涉及复杂的法律要求和合规义务。本报告基于对瑞士财富管理法律环境的深度分析，为华人高净值人士提供系统性的财富管理法律实务指南。

     

    As a global wealth management center, Switzerland has a comprehensive financial legal system and strict banking confidentiality system. For Chinese high-net-worth individuals, Switzerland provides high-quality wealth management services but also involves complex legal requirements and compliance obligations. This report is based on an in-depth analysis of Switzerland's wealth management legal environment, providing systematic wealth management legal practice guidelines for Chinese high-net-worth individuals.
