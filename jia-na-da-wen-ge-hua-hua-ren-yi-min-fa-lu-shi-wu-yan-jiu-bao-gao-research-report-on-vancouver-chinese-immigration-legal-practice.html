    一、研究背景与移民吸引力

    Research Background and Immigration Attractiveness

     

    温哥华作为加拿大重要城市和华人聚居地，拥有优质的生活环境、完善的社会福利和相对宽松的移民政策。对于华人移民而言，温哥华提供了得天独厚的移民优势，但移民法律体系复杂，申请程序严格。本报告基于对温哥华移民法律环境的深度分析，为华人移民提供系统性的移民法律实务指南。

     

    As an important Canadian city and Chinese settlement area, Vancouver has a high-quality living environment, comprehensive social welfare, and relatively relaxed immigration policies. For Chinese immigrants, Vancouver provides unique immigration advantages, but the immigration legal system is complex and application procedures are strict. This report is based on an in-depth analysis of Vancouver's immigration legal environment, providing systematic immigration legal practice guidelines for Chinese immigrants.
