    一、研究背景与金融科技中心地位

    Research Background and Fintech Center Status

     

    新加坡作为亚洲金融科技中心，拥有完善的金融监管体系、先进的科技基础设施和政府支持政策。对于华人金融科技投资者而言，新加坡提供了优质的金融科技投资环境，特别是在数字银行、支付服务、区块链等领域。本报告基于对新加坡金融科技投资法律环境的深度分析，为华人金融科技投资者提供系统性的投资法律实务指南。

     

    As an Asian fintech center, Singapore has a comprehensive financial regulatory system, advanced technology infrastructure, and government support policies. For Chinese fintech investors, Singapore provides a high-quality fintech investment environment, especially in fields such as digital banking, payment services, and blockchain. This report is based on an in-depth analysis of Singapore's fintech investment legal environment, providing systematic fintech investment legal practice guidelines for Chinese fintech investors.
