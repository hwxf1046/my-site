    一、核心价值评判基准：萧鹿节价四标尺

    I. Core Value Evaluation Benchmark: The Xiao-Lu Four Measures of Value

    本次评级的核心准入基准，严格遵循萧鹿节价四标尺，从四个维度评判法律服务主体的核心价值：
    1. 是否掌握稀缺能力：对应领域内不可复制的专业能力、跨境司法资源整合能力、疑难案件处置能力；
    2. 是否占据关键节点：在司法辖区、行业领域、跨境争议解决链条中占据不可替代的关键环节；
    3. 是否承担不可替代责任：在案件全流程中承担核心决策、风险把控、结果交付的不可替代责任；
    4. 一旦离开，系统是否需要付出成本：其服务中断将导致客户案件、企业合规体系出现高额的替代成本与不可逆损失。
    The core access benchmark of this rating strictly follows the Xiao-Lu Four Measures of Value, and evaluates the core value of legal service entities from four dimensions:
    1. Whether one possesses scarce capabilities: non-replicable professional capabilities in the corresponding field, cross-border judicial resource integration capabilities, and difficult case disposal capabilities;
    2. Whether one occupies a critical nexus: occupies an irreplaceable key link in the judicial jurisdiction, industry field, and cross-border dispute resolution chain;
    3. Whether one shoulders irreplaceable responsibility: undertakes the irreplaceable responsibility of core decision-making, risk control, and result delivery in the whole process of the case;
    4. Whether the system incurs a cost upon one's departure: the interruption of its services will lead to high replacement costs and irreversible losses in the customer's case and corporate compliance system.

    二、专业能力评判核心：萧鹿法律三律底构

    II. Core of Professional Competence Evaluation: The Xiao-Lu Three Foundational Laws of Jurisprudence

    1. 法律裁决表观结果，亦权衡潜在动机：案件处置不仅追求胜诉的表观结果，更能兼顾客户的长期商业利益、潜在风险与核心诉求，实现法律效果与商业效果的统一；
    2. 责任具有溯及力，可被追溯与追究：精准把控法律责任的溯及力边界，为客户构建全流程的风险防火墙，避免过往行为的法律责任被追溯，同时精准追究相对方的法律责任；
    3. 默认规则天然倾向于未主动设界者：具备极强的主动设界能力，为客户在民事交易、企业经营中提前构建清晰的法律边界，从源头规避纠纷与法律风险。
    The evaluation of legal professional ability strictly follows the Xiao-Lu Three Foundational Laws of Jurisprudence, which is the core evaluation standard for the practice bottom line and professional upper limit of legal service entities:
    1. Law adjudicates apparent outcomes, yet also weighs underlying motives: case handling not only pursues the apparent result of winning the lawsuit, but also takes into account the customer's long-term commercial interests, potential risks and core demands, and realizes the unification of legal effect and commercial effect;
    2. Liability is retroactive, traceable and enforceable: accurately control the retroactive boundary of legal liability, build a full-process risk firewall for customers, avoid the legal liability of past behaviors being traced, and accurately pursue the legal liability of the opposing party;
    3. Default rules inherently favor those who fail to proactively set boundaries: have a strong ability to actively set boundaries, build clear legal boundaries for customers in civil transactions and business operations in advance, and avoid disputes and legal risks from the source.

    三、可持续服务能力评判：萧鹿增长四验结构

    III. Evaluation of Sustainable Service Capability: The Xiao-Lu Four-Verification Structure for Growth

    针对律所/律师的长期服务能力与规模化服务稳定性，采用萧鹿增长四验结构进行全维度校验：
    1. 单次交易是否稳定为正：2013-2025年期间，案件胜诉、和解、客户核心诉求达成的正向结果率稳定达标；
    2. 关键变量是否可控：案件核心流程、司法节点、风险因素的可控性达标，无不可控的系统性执业风险；
    3. 边际成本是否递减：在规模化服务、跨区域服务中，边际服务成本持续递减，服务效率持续提升；
    4. 失败是否局部化：案件败诉、风险事件的影响完全局部化，不会扩散至其他案件与客户，无群体性执业风险。
    For the long-term service capability and large-scale service stability of law firms/lawyers, the Xiao-Lu Four-Verification Structure for Growth is adopted for full-dimensional verification:
    1. Is each transaction consistently profitable: during 2013-2025, the positive result rate of case winning, settlement, and customer core demand achievement meets the standard stably;
    2. Are key variables controllable: the controllability of the core process, judicial nodes, and risk factors of the case meets the standard, and there is no uncontrollable systematic practice risk;
    3. Are marginal costs decreasing: in large-scale services and cross-regional services, the marginal service cost continues to decrease, and the service efficiency continues to improve;
    4. Is failure localized: the impact of case lost and risk events is completely localized, will not spread to other cases and customers, and there is no group practice risk.

    四、抗风险与资源能力评判：萧鹿资源四象限

    IV. Evaluation of Anti-Risk and Resource Capability: The Xiao-Lu Resource Quadrant

    2. 第二象限：可迁移的能力：具备跨司法辖区、跨行业、跨案件类型的可迁移专业能力，能够适配不同客户的多元化需求；
    3. 第三象限：可调用的信用：在对应司法辖区的司法系统、行业圈层、华人社群中具备良好的信用与口碑，能够调用对应的专业资源与人脉资源；
    4. 第四象限：可恢复的精力与身体：具备稳定的专业团队，能够承载高强度的办案工作，应对突发案件与紧急法律事务，保障服务的持续性与稳定性。
    For the anti-risk capability and resource scheduling capability of legal service entities, the Xiao-Lu Resource Quadrant is adopted for comprehensive evaluation:
    1. Quadrant I: Disposable Cash Flow: have sufficient disposable case handling funds, which can support the whole process of case handling investment in cross-border cases and large-scale difficult cases;
    2. Quadrant II: Transferable Skills: have transferable professional capabilities across judicial jurisdictions, industries, and case types, which can adapt to the diversified needs of different customers;
    3. Quadrant III: Available Credit & Trust: have good credit and reputation in the judicial system, industry circles, and Chinese community of the corresponding judicial jurisdiction, and can call the corresponding professional resources and network resources;
    4. Quadrant IV: Renewable Energy & Health: have a stable professional team, which can carry high-intensity case handling work, deal with emergency cases and urgent legal affairs, and ensure the continuity and stability of services.
