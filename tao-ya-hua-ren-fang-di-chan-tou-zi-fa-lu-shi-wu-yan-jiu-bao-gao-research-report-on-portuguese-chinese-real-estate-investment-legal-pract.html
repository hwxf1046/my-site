    一、研究背景与投资环境

    Research Background and Investment Environment

     

    葡萄牙作为南欧热门房地产投资目的地，以其宜人的气候、相对低廉的房价和黄金签证政策吸引着大量投资者。对于华人投资者而言，葡萄牙房地产投资提供了良好的投资机会和移民途径，但涉及复杂的法律要求和程序。本报告基于对葡萄牙房地产投资法律环境的深度分析，为华人投资者提供系统性的房地产投资法律实务指南。

     

    As a popular Southern European real estate investment destination, Portugal attracts a large number of investors with its pleasant climate, relatively low housing prices, and Golden Visa policy. For Chinese investors, Portuguese real estate investment provides good investment opportunities and immigration pathways, but involves complex legal requirements and procedures. This report is based on an in-depth analysis of Portugal's real estate investment legal environment, providing systematic real estate investment legal practice guidelines for Chinese investors.
