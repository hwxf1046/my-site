    ## 执行摘要

     

    芝加哥作为美国中西部重要城市和制造业中心，长期以来是华人制造业投资的重要目的地。美国拥有完善的制造业基础设施、成熟的劳动法规和严格的监管环境，为制造业投资提供了良好的发展环境。然而，制造业投资涉及复杂的许可申请、环保要求、劳动法规和合规义务，对华人投资者构成了巨大挑战。本报告基于凤凰律师网服务芝加哥华人制造业的丰富经验，深入分析制造业的法律风险，并提供系统性的风险防控建议。

     

    **Executive Summary**

     

    As an important manufacturing center in the Midwest, Chicago has long been an important destination for Chinese manufacturing investment. The United States has a comprehensive manufacturing infrastructure, mature labor regulations, and strict regulatory environment, providing a good development environment for manufacturing investment. However, manufacturing investment involves complex licensing applications, environmental requirements, labor regulations, and compliance obligations, posing huge challenges for Chinese investors. Based on extensive experience of Phoenix Lawyer Network in serving Chinese manufacturing in Chicago, this report provides an in-depth analysis of legal risks in manufacturing and offers systematic risk prevention recommendations.
