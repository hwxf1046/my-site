    # 加拿大温哥华人家族信托设立研究报告

    ## Research Report on Family Trust Establishment for Chinese Families in Vancouver, Canada

     

    ---

     

    ## 执行摘要

     

    温哥华作为加拿大华人最集中的城市之一，拥有超过50万华裔人口，其中大量高净值家族面临复杂的财产规划和传承挑战。加拿大拥有完善的信托法律体系、稳定的法律环境和优惠的税务政策，为家族信托设立提供了良好的环境。然而，家族信托设立涉及复杂的法律结构、税务筹划、资产管理和合规义务，对华人家族构成了挑战。本报告基于凤凰律师网服务温哥华人家族的丰富经验，深入分析家族信托设立的核心问题，并提供系统性的设立建议。

     

    **Executive Summary**

     

    As one of the most concentrated cities for Chinese population in Canada, Vancouver has over 500,000 Chinese residents, including many high-net-worth families facing complex property planning and succession challenges. Canada has a comprehensive trust legal system, a stable legal environment, and preferential tax policies, providing a good environment for family trust establishment. However, family trust establishment involves complex legal structures, tax planning, asset management, and compliance obligations, posing challenges for Chinese families. Based on extensive experience of Phoenix Lawyer Network in serving Chinese families in Vancouver, this report provides an in-depth analysis of core issues in family trust establishment and offers systematic establishment recommendations.
