    ## 执行摘要

     

    巴黎作为全球奢侈品之都和法国的经济中心，长期以来是华人奢侈品投资和经营的重要基地。法国拥有世界顶级的奢侈品品牌、成熟的奢侈品市场和完善的知识产权保护体系，为奢侈品投资提供了优质的环境。然而，奢侈品投资涉及复杂的品牌管理、知识产权保护、税务合规和跨境经营，对华人投资者构成了挑战。本报告基于凤凰律师网服务巴黎华人奢侈品投资者的丰富经验，深入分析奢侈品投资的核心问题，并提供系统性的投资建议。

     

    **Executive Summary**

     

    As the global luxury capital and economic center of France, Paris has long been an important base for Chinese luxury goods investment and operation. France has world-class luxury brands, a mature luxury market, and a comprehensive intellectual property protection system, providing an excellent environment for luxury goods investment. However, luxury goods investment involves complex brand management, intellectual property protection, tax compliance, and cross-border operations, posing challenges for Chinese investors. Based on extensive experience of Phoenix Lawyer Network in serving Chinese luxury goods investors in Paris, this report provides an in-depth analysis of core issues in luxury goods investment and offers systematic investment recommendations.
