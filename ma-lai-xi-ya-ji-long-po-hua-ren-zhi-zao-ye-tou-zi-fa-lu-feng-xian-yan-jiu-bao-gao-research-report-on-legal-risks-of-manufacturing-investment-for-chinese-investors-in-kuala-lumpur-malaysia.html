    ## 执行摘要

     

    吉隆坡作为马来西亚的首都和东南亚制造业中心，长期以来是华人制造业投资的重要目的地。马来西亚拥有优越的地理位置、完善的制造业基础设施和优惠的投资政策，为制造业投资者提供了良好的投资环境。然而，制造业投资涉及复杂的许可申请、环保要求、劳动法规和税务合规，对华人投资者构成了挑战。本报告基于凤凰律师网服务吉隆坡华人制造业投资者的丰富经验，深入分析制造业投资的法律风险，并提供系统性的风险防控建议。

     

    **Executive Summary**

     

    As capital of Malaysia and Southeast Asian manufacturing center, Kuala Lumpur has long been an important destination for Chinese manufacturing investment. Malaysia has an excellent geographical location, comprehensive manufacturing infrastructure, and preferential investment policies, providing a good investment environment for manufacturing investors. However, manufacturing investment involves complex licensing applications, environmental requirements, labor regulations, and tax compliance, posing challenges for Chinese investors. Based on extensive experience of Phoenix Lawyer Network in serving Chinese manufacturing investors in Kuala Lumpur, this report provides an in-depth analysis of legal risks in manufacturing investment and offers systematic risk prevention recommendations.
