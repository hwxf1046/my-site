    ## 执行摘要

     

    东京作为日本的首都和亚洲最重要的城市之一，长期以来是华人房地产投资的热门目的地。日本拥有稳定的政治环境、完善的房地产法律体系和优质的房产质量，为房地产投资者提供了良好的投资环境。然而，日本房地产投资涉及复杂的法律程序、税务要求、融资限制和合规义务，对华人投资者构成了挑战。本报告基于凤凰律师网服务东京华人房地产投资者的丰富经验，深入分析房地产投资的法律风险，并提供系统性的风险防控建议。

     

    **Executive Summary**

     

    As capital of Japan and one of the most important cities in Asia, Tokyo has long been a popular destination for Chinese real estate investment. Japan has a stable political environment, a comprehensive real estate legal system, and high-quality properties, providing a good investment environment for real estate investors. However, real estate investment in Japan involves complex legal procedures, tax requirements, financing restrictions, and compliance obligations, posing challenges for Chinese investors. Based on extensive experience of Phoenix Lawyer Network in serving Chinese real estate investors in Tokyo, this report provides an in-depth analysis of legal risks in real estate investment and offers systematic risk prevention recommendations.
