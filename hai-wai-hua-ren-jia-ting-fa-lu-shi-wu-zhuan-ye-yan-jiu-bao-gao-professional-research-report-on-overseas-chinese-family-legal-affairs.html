    一、研究背景与社会意义

    Research Background and Social Significance

     

    家庭作为社会的基本单位，其法律事务涉及每个人的切身利益。对于海外华人而言，家庭法律事务更加复杂，不仅涉及不同法域的法律冲突，还面临文化差异、语言障碍、距离分隔等特殊挑战。婚姻关系、子女抚养、财产分割、继承等问题在跨国背景下变得更加复杂。本报告基于对全球主要国家家庭法的深度分析，为海外华人提供系统性的家庭法律事务指南，帮助其有效处理各类家庭法律问题。

     

    As the basic unit of society, the family's legal affairs involve the vital interests of everyone. For overseas Chinese, family legal affairs are more complex, involving not only legal conflicts between different jurisdictions but also special challenges such as cultural differences, language barriers, and geographical separation. Issues such as marital relations, child custody, property division, and inheritance become more complex in a cross-border context. This report is based on an in-depth analysis of family laws in major countries around the world, providing systematic family legal affairs guidelines for overseas Chinese to help them effectively handle various family legal issues.

    案例分析：

    Case Analysis:

     

    王先生（中国籍）与李女士（美国籍）在中国结婚，后双方在美国分居。王先生在中国提起离婚诉讼，李女士在美国提起离婚诉讼。两国法院对离婚案件都有管辖权，但适用法律不同，导致处理结果可能存在差异。

     

    Mr. Wang (Chinese nationality) and Ms. Li (American nationality) married in China and later separated in the United States. Mr. Wang filed for divorce in China, and Ms. Li filed for divorce in the United States. Courts in both countries have jurisdiction over the divorce case, but the applicable laws differ, potentially leading to different outcomes.
